Biography

I am a researcher at Innovation Labs, Tata Consultancy Services. My research interests lie in the intersection of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics and Deep Learning with an application domain of Augmented and Virtual Reality. I completed my undergrad with Computer Science and Engineering major at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi (IIIT-Delhi).

I am passionate about problem solving in general. My passion to pursue a career in research in computer science and mathematics stems from the direct impact it has on human life. This, I feel, is a thrilling and an exciting way to lead a life. My other interests that fascinate me the most include music and travelling.
